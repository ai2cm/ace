**For most other variables, the spectral differences are small and comparable across all period boundaries:**
- `air_temperature_7`, `specific_total_water_7`, `PRESsfc`, `TMP2m` — all show RMSE < 0.016 for any period pair, meaning their spectra are quite stable over time
- `eastward_wind_0` — shows moderate differences (RMSE ~0.03-0.07), with a noticeable increase in small-scale power post-2010 (1.18x vs 1979-2000)
- `air_temperature_0` — moderate differences; interestingly the 2010-2022 vs 2001-2009 jump (RMSE 0.059) is *larger* than the 2010-2022 vs 1979-2000 jump (RMSE 0.031), suggesting some spectral change around 2010

**On the 2010 boundary specifically:**
- For `air_temperature_0` and `eastward_wind_0`, the 2001-2009 period looks more different from the neighboring periods than you'd expect, consistent with the 2010 stream boundary having an effect on upper-atmosphere variables
- For `specific_total_water_0`, the 2001-2009 period actually has *more* small-scale power than 2010-2022 (ratio 0.79x), which is unusual

**Bottom line:** The pre-1979 regime shift dominates spectral differences for `specific_total_water_0` (the variable with the largest change).
